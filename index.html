Vision,Culture and People focused recruitment

As your hiring needs continue to evolve - so should your recruitment partner.
Every team looks, behaves and measures differently
Our dedicated executive recruiters come with more than 80 years of collective experience and take a unique approach to every client, helping to identify culturally- aligned leadership to drive your mission.

Executive search

We specialise in recruitment of candidates at mid-level, senior and executive leadership levels. We uncover the personality and philosophy of leaders, to better connect them with your organisation.

HR Recruiting

The Prime HR team has partnered with HR professionals for more than 3 decades and built a strong network of HR professionals. Our experience has shown that no organisation or candidate is the same. 

Finance Recruiting

We understand that finance teams are at the core of the smooth functioning of organisations. We take special care to ensure candidates are checked for utmost professionalism and integrity. 

Sales Recruiting

We target recruit top sales performers with the exact background you are looking for. Most sales hires are head hunted and are veted through a rigorous process.
